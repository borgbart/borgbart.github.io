Design: Alessandro Dubini

A concept born from the desire to use an unconventional material as a protagonist to make handles: concrete. The almost ancestral production recalls the ancient technique of forging with molds and casting of a solid body: the final aesthetic result of Juno- this is the name chosen for the new handle- is of great visual impact, but at the same time the design is very clean and minimalist. Winner of GOOD DESIGN AWARD 2017.
