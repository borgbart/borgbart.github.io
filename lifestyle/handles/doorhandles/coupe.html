Design: Dodo Arslan

Coupé was born from an essential idea, to obtain two handles by cutting a standard bar obliquely. Dodo Arslan, during the lockdowns and in the face of the sharp increase in the costs of raw materials and energy, set itself the goal of creating a handle that would reduce the use of material as much as possible and at the same time eliminate some steps of the traditional process production of handles.
