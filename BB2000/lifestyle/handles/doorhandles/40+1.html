Design: Lorenzo Ponzelli / Francesco Valentini

Made by Lorenzo Ponzelli and Francesco Valentini who worked on the concept of clean lines but present with character, trying to make it elegant with simplicity. For this reason we made the tapered shape from the base point where it’s fixed to the door, up to the tip, and at the same time folded inside so as to play with lights and shadows to represent the cut facet of the precious stones. The tip, on the other hand, perfectly shares the 40 degree inclination of the Eclisse 40 frame. The upper and lower inward fold gives an excellent ergonomic grip, which makes it almost unique in the panorama of squared lined handles.
