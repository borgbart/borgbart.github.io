Design: Alessandro Dubini

The approach to this project was certainly nonconformist, far from the limits and traditional associations, but rethinking and re imagining the conventions and use of this object. Simple and clean lines converge to form a minimalist whole. Visually light and essential, “K” is an object with an innovative and ingenious design, but above all with great functionality that allows different ways of fixing and use.
