The VITRO 2M2S desk combines modern design with advanced functionality. Its elegant glass top (120x60 cm) in RAL 9017, Traffic black, adds a sleek look to any office or home workspace. With electric height adjustment (710 mm to 1210 mm), it offers the flexibility to work seated or standing.

With a load capacity of 100 kg, the desk is stable even when adjusting heights. The integrated control panel in the glass top features 3 memory keys, allowing you to save your preferred settings for easy access. You can charge your devices wirelessly (10W) or via 2x USB-A and 1x USB-C ports built into the desk.

Safety is key, with a child safety lock and an anti-collision function that ensure safe and secure use. The VITRO 2M2S desk is a perfect blend of style, functionality, and peace of mind for your workspace.
