The UPLIGHT One BF Clip delivers all the intelligent lighting performance of the original floor version—without taking up any floor space. Mounted directly to your desk via a sturdy clip, it’s the ideal solution for compact work environments or minimalist setups.

With ultra-low glare (UGR ≤ 6) and biodynamic light color ranging from 3000K to 6000K, this lamp adapts to your daily rhythm, enhancing concentration and comfort. Intelligent sensors detect motion, distance, and ambient light to automatically adjust brightness throughout your workday.

The high-performance LED system, powered by Philips SMD3030 chips and a flicker-free Boke driver, provides ≥7,800 lumens of efficient, balanced light (30% direct / 70% indirect), ensuring both task and ambient illumination. Its slim, clean design keeps your desktop clutter-free while delivering over 36,000 hours of powerful, ergonomic lighting.
